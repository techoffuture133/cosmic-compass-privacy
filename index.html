Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Cosmic Compass: Tarot & Horoscope is a paid mobile application available on the Google Play Store as a one-time purchase. The app contains no advertisements, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) we collect when you use the app and how we handle it. We are committed to protecting your privacy and collect only the minimum data necessary for the app to function.

2. Authentication with Google Sign-In

The app uses Sign in with Google to let you log in quickly and securely.

When you sign in, Google may share the following information with the app:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Profile picture
  • Google User ID

This data is used only for:

  • Authentication / login
  • Basic personalization (e.g. displaying your name or profile picture if you choose)

We do not store any of this information on our servers. All authentication is handled directly by Google, and the data remains only temporarily in your device's memory during the active session.

When you log out or close the app, this data is cleared from the app’s local memory.

For details on how Google handles your data, please read: Google Privacy Policy

3. Other Data We Collect

Apart from the Google Sign-In data described above, the app collects no other personal information.

  • No location tracking
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No usage statistics sent to third parties
  • No behavioral profiling

Any preferences you set (zodiac sign, birth date, favorite tarot deck, etc.) are stored exclusively locally on your device.

4. Data Retention

Google Sign-In data exists only during your active session and is removed when you log out or close the app. Locally stored preferences remain on your device until you:

  • Log out
  • Clear app data
  • Uninstall the app

No personal data is kept on any external servers controlled by us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose any user data to third parties, except:

  • Google – only as part of the Sign in with Google authentication flow (governed by Google’s privacy policy)

6. Payments

All payments are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing. We never see or store your credit card details, payment method, or any financial information.

7. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws)

Depending on your location you may have the right to:

  • Revoke Google Sign-In access at any time via Google Account permissions
  • Request a copy of data the app uses (contact us via email below)
  • Delete locally stored preferences by logging out, clearing app data, or uninstalling

Because we do not store personal data on servers, there is no server-side data to delete.

8. Children’s Privacy

Cosmic Compass: Tarot & Horoscope is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available in the app and on this page. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
